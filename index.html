Services

It's my goal to create clean, intuitive, and accessible websites that deliver value for your business or project. Here are some ways I get this done:

  • Building with a "Mobile First" approach: Mobile devices have become the most common medium for viewing websites. Ensuring that your website looks good and is easy to use on a mobile device is imperative.
  • Intuitive User Interface: It's important that your website not only looks good, but also makes sense. I like to think about the small decisions that make a big impact, like where a "call to action" button may be placed to ensure your customers and stakeholders are getting the core benefits of your product or service.
  • Accessibility: What good is an awesome website if it can't be used by everyone? I try my best to make sure that your website is usable by all no matter their personal ability to experience it. Everyone should be able experience the same feeling when visiting you online.

What's the process like?

The process is easy!

  1. Consultation & Proposal: We'll start with a conversation where you can tell me all about your project needs and the problems you're aiming to solve. I'll then develop a detailed proposal based on our conversation that will go over all requirements of the the project and an action plan.
  2. Development: The project will be built based on the above. Clients will receive regular status updates along the way.
  3. Launch: Your site is live! Upon release of the finished project, I'll educate you on any nuances with your site and will go over any concerns you may have.
